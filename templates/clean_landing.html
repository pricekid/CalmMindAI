How It Works

It's Personal

Journal your thoughts and feelings in a private, secure space that's always there when you need it. Dear Teddy provides a judgment-free zone for self-expression.

It's Supportive

Receive personalized insights and reflections that help you gain clarity and perspective. Our AI companion provides CBT-based support to guide your mental wellness journey.

It's Insightful

Track your emotional patterns over time and identify triggers that affect your mental wellness. Visualize your progress and celebrate your growth along the way.

It's Private

Your data is encrypted and secure. We prioritize your privacy and confidentiality, ensuring you have a safe space to explore your thoughts and feelings.
