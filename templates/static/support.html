Crisis Support

Important Notice

Dear Teddy is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Crisis Centers

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical difficulties:

  1. Try refreshing your browser
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Try using a different browser
  5. Contact our support team if the issue persists
