Crisis Resources

It's okay to ask for help when you're feeling overwhelmed. Reaching out is a sign of strength, not weakness. You don't have to face difficult moments alone.

Lifeline Barbados

536-4500

Free, confidential support from trained counselors. Available 24/7 for anyone experiencing emotional distress or thoughts of suicide.

Crisis Text Line (International)

Text HOME to 741741

Available in US, Canada, UK, and Ireland. Free, 24/7 text support with trained crisis counselors.
